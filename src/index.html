What is Kennekt

Kennekt is an easy system that quickly connects clients to businesses and businesses to clients. As a client, you've probably been put on hold for a long time, transferred to the wrong department or weren't able to reach who you wanted. Businesses can quickly create a mobile app presence using Kennekt. A business would quickly register and identify what its departments and sub-departments are. Within minutes, a business can offer its clients a higher level of service and quick communication. Having a quick pulse on your clients will surely enhance your clients experience and satisfaction.
